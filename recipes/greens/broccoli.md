## Spicy Crispy Broccoli

### Ingredients:
(serves 1)

- 2 Broccoli Crowns
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp onion powder
- Chilli flakes
- 2 tsp reduced sodium soy sauce

### Method:

Cook garlic in olive oil over medium heat.
Add broccoli, onion powder, soy sauce, and chilli flakes.
Cook for 5 - 8 minutes until cooked but still crisp.
